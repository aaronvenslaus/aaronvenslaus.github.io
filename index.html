MODERN GUIDE TO BUY A CAR


Introduction Electric Diesel Gasoline

This website will be useful for users who decide to purchase a car. One of the important factors to consider while purchasing a car is to consider the miles per gallon depending on the car type.

Based on the type of fuel interested, users can naviagte to the appropirate page by clicking the navigator page on the top.

Within every page, there will be a graph with the manufactures on the x-axis and average MPG on the y-axis. Using this graph users can arrive at a decision on which car to purchase. There are options within every page where the user can choose between Average Highway MPG and Average City MPG.

In addition to that, in the Gasoline webpage there is an additional option to choose the number of cyclinders present in the engine.
