Introduction Electric Diesel Gasoline

Welcome to the modern guide to buy a car. One of the important factors to consider while purchasing a car is to consider the miles per gallon depending on the car type.

Based on the type of fuel interested, please naviagte to the appropirate page by clicking the navigator page on the top.

Within every page, there will be a graph with the manufacturers on the x-axis and average MPG on the y-axis. Using this graph please arrive at a decision on which car to purchase. There are options within every page where you can choose between Average Highway MPG and Average City MPG.

In addition, inside the Gasoline webpage there is an additional option to choose the number of cyclinders present in the engine.
